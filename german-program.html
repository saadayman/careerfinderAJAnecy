Education

Studying in Germany in most German universities is funded by the government, as it works to recruit students from all over the world. Where a decree was issued by the government to cancel all tuition fees in universities in 2014 However, this abolition of fees does not affect higher education and private universities.

The student at the bachelor’s stage may pay symbolic amounts to cover the expenses of services provided by the university and the expenses of registration fees, which are symbolic amounts ranging from (150 to 250) only. For foreign students, this decision was a golden opportunity to complete their studies abroad for free.

Since the study in Germany is done in the German language, those wishing to study must pass one of the international exams for the German language, namely A1, A2, B1, B2 where they must meet the condition of the degree of admission to the university they wish to join, which is the average level in most.

Why should a group of foreign students study the preparatory year before starting their university studies, and the student learns in this year the basic materials that qualify to study the required branch.

The Preparatory College prepares you technically and linguistically in an international school before studying at a German university if your previous education is not sufficient for direct study. Most universities take a two-semester preparatory course Participation in the assessment test is also possible after one semester if the applicant shows very good performance in the first semester.
